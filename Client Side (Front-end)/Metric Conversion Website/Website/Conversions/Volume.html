Volume Conversion Description

Among the requirements of traders or tax collectors, a reliable standard of volume is the hardest to achieve. Nature provides some very rough averages, such as goatskins. Baskets, sacks or pottery jars can be made to approximately consistent sizes, sufficient perhaps for many everyday transactions. But where the exact amount of any commodity needs to be known, weight is the measure more likely to be relied upon than volume.
